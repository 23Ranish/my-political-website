Building a Better Canada

Join our movement for strong values and a prosperous future.

🏠 Housing

  • Remove red tape and gatekeepers that slow down development.
  • Withhold funds from cities that don’t meet housing goals.
  • Reward cities that hit home-building targets with more investment.

🌍 Climate Change

  • Prioritize human lives and community resilience over politics.
  • End punishing carbon taxes that hurt families and small businesses.
  • Promote clean innovation to reduce emissions and grow jobs.

🩺 Healthcare

  • Hire 15,000 new doctors by 2030 to improve access.
  • Maintain and strengthen provincial healthcare systems.
  • Modernize digital health access for faster, smarter care.

💼 Work & Economy

  • Lower taxes so hard work pays off more than ever.
  • End the “Just Transition” to protect jobs and industries.
  • Boost wages through economic competitiveness and small business support.
